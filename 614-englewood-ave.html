Forest Green Apartments is Amherst's northern gateway - a vibrant apartment community with well-known shopping next door and convenient access to downtown Buffalo within 20 minutes. Nearby workplaces are easy to reach and at the end of the day, Forest Green residents can retreat to a welcome enclave.

Modern, elegant apartments share amenities from granite countertops, large walk-in closets, high-end cabinetry with crown moulding, stainless steel appliances, garbage disposals, to name just a few. At Forest Green you'll enjoy a valued sense of being right where you belong. Choose to live where convenience and quiet living meet.
